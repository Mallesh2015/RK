Careers

At RK Associates, we believe in nurturing young legal minds by providing hands-on experience in the legal profession. Our internship program is designed for law students who seek practical exposure to various aspects of legal practice, including litigation, corporate law, and research.

Why Intern with Us?

✔ Real-world legal experience

Work on live cases and gain practical insights into legal proceedings

✔ Mentorship from experts

Learn directly from seasoned legal professionals and gain valuable industry knowledge.

✔ Research & Drafting Skills

Enhance your legal research, drafting, and case analysis abilities.

✔ Court Exposure

Get an opportunity to observe court proceedings and understand litigation strategies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Law students (3rd-year and above for 5-year courses, 2nd-year and above for 3-year courses)
  • Strong research and analytical skills
  • Eagerness to learn and contribute

How to Apply?

Interested candidates can apply by sending their resume and cover letter to support@RKlegalexpert.com with the subject line: Internship Application – [Your Name]. Join us and take the first step towards a successful legal career with RK Associate!
